Academic Timetable

  

Dalhousie offers courses at two main locations, Halifax and Truro. The Truro location is home to our Faculty of Agriculture and is located about 100 km from Halifax. Distance courses are delivered to students through a medium where students are not physically present in a traditional setting such as a classroom. "Other" refers to courses held at locations other than Halifax or Truro. The filter options can be used to search on a location or combination of locations e.g. Halifax and Distance will show all the Halifax or Distance courses offered for a subject.

The Faculty of Agriculture Technology Program courses are included in the timetable with course numbers between 0100-0299 and are only open to Technology students.

Use the radio buttons below to select a term and location. Click submit to see the list of subjects. Click on the subject link to view the courses offered.

Periodically, the timetable may appear with all listings marked "C/D" (Consult Department). This occurs during a weekly refresh of scheduling information. Please check back in 10-15 minutes to view your timetable.
