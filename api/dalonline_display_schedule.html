Academic Timetable

  



Please read the following information carefully before using the Academic Timetable:

Delivery Methods: As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, Dalhousie has chosen to move instruction online for the majority of Fall 2020 term courses. Online courses will be delivered in three potential ways, which you will see indicated on the timetable:

1. ASYNCHRONOUS (recorded) courses do not require you to be online at any specific time/day.

2. SYNCHRONOUS (live) courses require that you join the class online at a specific time/day every week. Courses that are delivered synchronously are scheduled in Atlantic Time.

3. Some courses will be delivered using BOTH asynchronous and synchronous methods in combination. These courses require that you join the class online at a specific time/day as indicated, but not exclusively. Courses that are delivered in this format will display multiple lines indicating the dates for any scheduled synchronous sessions and will also indicate that the course has some asynchronous components.

C/D, or Consult Department, shows on the timetable whenever there is no specific information to list. You can consult the department offering the course for answers to any questions you might have about how the course will be delivered or wait to receive your syllabus once the course begins.


Navigating: To navigate the page, use the buttons below to select a term and location. Summer and Fall 2020 courses have moved to online teaching. They are classified as “Online” on this timetable. Selecting Halifax, Truro or Other will NOT bring these courses up. The best way to ensure you are seeing all courses within a department is to select “ALL” under Location.


Updates:The Fall 2020 timetable is being updated regularly to reflect online delivery. Changes will be made over the next several weeks. Please continue to check the timetable for the most up to date information.
